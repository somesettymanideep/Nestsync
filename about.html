Who we are

Our Story .

In the bustling world of smart home automation, where convenience meets innovation, there lies a company dedicated to transforming living spaces into havens of comfort and style. Founded in 2021, our journey began with a vision to revolutionize everyday living through cutting-edge automation technology. Today, we proudly stand at the forefront of the automation industry, consistently delivering seamless and efficient solutions to our clients across India and beyond.

At the heart of our company is a deep-rooted vision to enhance the way people interact with their homes. We recognized the potential of automation technology to simplify tasks, enhance security, and elevate aesthetics. With this vision in mind, we embarked on our journey to create smart home solutions that cater to the diverse needs of modern living.

Super Team .

At Nestsync, we're proud to have assembled a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about transforming ordinary living spaces into extraordinary homes. Our team is the backbone of our company, and each member plays a vital role in ensuring that we deliver the highest quality products and services to our customers.
